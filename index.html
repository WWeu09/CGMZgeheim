About Viviana's Nail Glam

Discover our story, our passion for nail artistry, and our commitment to excellence.

Our Story

Founded by Viviana, a passionate nail artist with over 10 years of experience, Viviana's Nail Glam was born from a desire to create a premium nail care experience that combines luxury, artistry, and personalized service.

What started as a small passion project has grown into a beloved salon known for its attention to detail, use of premium products, and commitment to creating a relaxing and indulgent experience for every client.

At Viviana's Nail Glam, we believe that nail care is not just about beauty—it's about self-care, expression, and confidence. Our mission is to help every client feel pampered, beautiful, and confident from the moment they walk through our doors.
